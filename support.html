Frequently Asked
How do I reset my transaction PIN?
Go to Dashboard → Settings → Security → Change Transaction PIN. You'll need to verify via your registered email or phone number.
Why is my card declined?
Check if your card is frozen, your spending limit is exceeded, or international payments are disabled. You can manage all settings in the Cards section.
How long do wire transfers take?
Domestic transfers typically arrive same day. International wire transfers take 1–3 business days depending on the destination country and receiving bank.
Is my money FDIC insured?
Yes. All deposits are FDIC-insured up to $250,000 per depositor. Your money is protected even if the bank were to fail.
